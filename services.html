Our Services

Providing inclusive, person-centered support for individuals with disabilities and behavioral health needs.

Individualized Family Support Living

We provide customized support services that empower individuals with disabilities to live with their families or in family-like settings. Our approach focuses on enhancing independence, building life skills, and promoting meaningful community involvement—all while respecting the unique needs, values, and goals of each individual and their family. With dedicated guidance and flexible care, we help create stable, nurturing environments where people can thrive.

Community Participation Services

Our Community Participation Services are designed to help individuals with disabilities actively engage in their communities through meaningful activities and social connections. We support each person in exploring interests, developing relationships, building vocational and daily living skills, and accessing local resources. Whether through volunteering, recreation, education, or employment pathways, our goal is to foster inclusion, independence, and a strong sense of belonging.

Respite Services

Our Respite Services offer temporary, quality care for individuals with disabilities, giving families and caregivers the time they need to rest, recharge, or attend to other responsibilities. Whether planned or on short notice, our flexible and supportive respite options ensure individuals receive safe, compassionate care in a familiar or trusted environment. We tailor each experience to meet the unique needs of the individual while promoting comfort, dignity, and continuity of care.

Nursing Services

Our Nursing Services provide comprehensive, person-centered medical care to individuals with physical, intellectual, or developmental disabilities. Delivered by licensed professionals, our care includes health assessments, medication administration, chronic condition management, and coordination with physicians and other healthcare providers. We focus on promoting wellness, safety, and dignity—ensuring each individual receives the medical support they need to thrive in their chosen environment.
